Who Are We?

We are Whiskers and Brews! We were founded in 2021 after the closure of Kingston Animal Shelter, we couldn't let all the cats go! We decided to follow our dreams and open up a cafe where 80% of our profits go towards the kitties! Stop by today to have a yummy drink and meet some fuzzy friends who are also up for adoption!
